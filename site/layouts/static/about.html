Our team

Streamlit was started by senior engineers, business people, and researchers from Google, Google X, and Twitter who have experience building large-scale products and well known open-source projects.

Adrien Treuille

Dr. Treuille has been a Zoox VP, Google X project lead, and Computer Science faculty at Carnegie Mellon. He has won numerous scientific awards, including the MIT TR35. Adrien has been featured in the documentaries What Will the Future Be Like by PBS/NOVA, and Lo and Behold by Werner Herzog.

Thiago Teixeira

Founder of Hangouts Chat at Google, and former tech lead manager of Hangouts web clients. Former tech lead of an AI project at Google X. He has a Ph.D. from Yale, and his research includes human behavior recognition in sensor networks and building a sensor-fusion middleware for the internet of things.

Amanda Kelly

Amanda has a range of product, operations and business experience spanning autonomous vehicles, NLP, AI, e-commerce and Android. Amanda was Director of Launch Operations at Zoox and before that she led product and operations for a handful of projects at Google X and Google machine learning. She has degrees from Stanford GSB and University of Notre Dame.

Nodira Khoussainova

Dr. Khoussainova was tech lead of Product Insights & Experimentation at Twitter. She has a Ph.D. from University of Washington, is the author of Lectures on Discrete Mathematics for Computer Science, and graduated from the University of Auckland at age 16.

Armando Atienza

Backend and Systems engineer with over 15 years of experience working on infrastructure projects at Google, Google X, SpaceX, healthcare.gov, Hillary for America, and as an internal in house consultant for Playground Global.

Tim Conkling

Tim is an independent game designer and programmer. He was a lead designer and engineer at Three Rings/SEGA and technical director at Gamelab NYC. His most recent project is “Antihero”, an award-winning multiplayer game about running a thieves’ guild in a gas-lit, Dickensian underworld.
